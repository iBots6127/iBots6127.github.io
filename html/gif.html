Girls in FIRST

During the 2012-2013 season, our team noticed a significant drop in female participation in the transition from FLL to FTC and FRC. Changing this pattern has been our main passion ever since.

What We've Done:
Given talks at Rockwood School District schools
Collected surveys over the course of three years
Started TeamFinder program
